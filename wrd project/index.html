About Konjic

Konjic is a town and center of similar commune on extreme north mountainous Herzegovina, at the central part of B&H. Agricultural and forestry land, huge water potential, great reserves of mineral of pirofilit are the main titles of huge richness of this part of BiH.

Konjic is attractive far more by its abundance of natural beauty that completely surrounds the town. The town is one of the oldest permanent settlements in Bosnia and Herzegovina, dating back almost 4000 years; the city in its current incarnation arising as an important town in the late 14th century.

"A place where Herzegovina merges with Bosnia"

Rafting

Rafting is a very adventurous activity and one of the most popular forms of active relaxation in Konjic, that opens the challenges of whitewater to both beginners and experienced rafters.

Hiking

Step out into the fresh mountain air at the most beautiful viewpoints. Prenj, Visočica, Čvrsnica, Lukomir are just a few places whose magnificent views you will enjoy during the hike.

Mountain biking

Enjoy MTB adventures in mountain areas, where you will be amazed with untouched nature while going off the beathen paths with beautiful views on the valleys and the surroundings.

Culture

Ciy tour includes visits to cultural and historical heritage sites of Konjic – Old Stone bridge, woodcarving museums, tombstones, Heritage museum, Tito's bunker and more beautiful places in Konjic.
